Research Interests

Evaluation of Human-machine Interaction

in Intelligent Cockpits

  • Based on human-machine interaction design theory, contextual experiments, and user interviews, we have established a HMI evaluation system for intelligent cockpits, providing a reference for the evaluation of automotive intelligent cockpit design for China Automotive Innovation Corporation.
  • Outcome: A HMI evaluation system tailored for intelligent cockpits has been established and utilized for the interaction evaluation of Ideal L9 and VOYAH Free, resulting in evaluation reports.

Research on the determination method of optimal virtual hand interaction zone in virtual 3D interface

  • We established an experimental space based on ergonomics, established an evaluation system and experimental methods from two dimensions of interactive performance and subjective preferences, determine the research process according to the HMI experimental process, and finally propose a method for determining the optimal interaction zone of virtual hands in virtual space.
  • Outcome: The method obtained from the research has been published as an invention patent.
